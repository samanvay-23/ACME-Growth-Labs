# Operations Strategy – Acme Growth Labs

## 1. Overview
The Operations team focuses on optimizing internal workflows, improving cross-team collaboration, and ensuring timely delivery of projects.

Key priorities:
- Efficiency
- Process standardization
- Risk management

---

## 2. Goals & Metrics
- Reduce process bottlenecks by 15%
- Improve on-time delivery (80% → 95%)
- Reduce support ticket resolution time to 24 hours
- Achieve 90% SOP compliance
- Improve operational cost efficiency by 10%

- Cross-team collaboration protocols

- Resource allocation and scheduling
- Process documentation and updates
