 Marketing Strategy

## 1. Overview
Q2 will focus on improving customer acquisition efficiency and scaling high-performing channels.

In Q1, we observed an 18% increase in CPA, driven by rising ad costs on Meta and underperforming creative.

Primary goal:
- Stabilize CPA
- Increase overall lead volume through better channel allocation and creative testing

---

## 2. Goals & KPIs
- Increase qualified leads by 25% (1,200 → 1,500/month)
- Reduce CPA from ₹1,450 to ₹1,200
- Improve landing page conversion rate from 2.8% to 3.5%
- Achieve 15% growth in marketing-attributed revenue

---

## 3. Target Audience
- Early-stage startups (Seed to Series B)
- Founders and growth leads in tech companies

### Markets
- India
- Southeast Asia

### Personas
- Founder: decision-maker, cost-sensitive  
- Growth Manager: performance-focused, data-driven  

---

## 4. Channels & Campaigns
### Paid
- Meta Ads
- Google Ads
- YouTube Ads

### Organic
- LinkedIn content
- SEO blogs

### Email
- Lead nurturing sequences

### Key Initiatives
- Shift 30% of Meta budget to YouTube
- Launch 2 new landing pages
- Increase LinkedIn output (3 → 5 posts/week)
